We are Warriors with an Aspiration to Conquer

Our School

Kambiri Victory High School is a True Testament to the Goodness and Faithfulness of God. Born of Faith, God Has Been Faithful in providing resources to facilitate Free High-Quality Education, Extra-Curriculum Activities, Facilities and Curriculum Practical Resources to Orphans or Underprivileged students, and also Affordable Education to Students all over the county

Our Vision

Is To Be The Preferred And Leading Private School That Empowers Students Through Provision Of An Education That Is All-round And Of Highest Quality

Our Mission

Is To Provide Adequate Infrastructure And Highly Motivated Staff Who Inspire And Challenge Students To Aspire To Academic Excellence Through Teaching And Mentoring

What We Offer

Computer Studies

Student's Interested in Pursuing Computer Studies Have an Opportunity at Victory Academy High School

Sciences

We offer classroom Chemistry, Biology and Physics and practical applications to Our students in modernized and fully stocked Laboratories

Other Courses

Our Agriculture Students are offered firsthand experience to curriculum projects like Green House Farming & Zero Grazing. Our Students are also offered Business Studies. This is in addition to all courses required by the ministry of education

Our Extra-Curriculum Activities

Just As Not All Angels Play the Harp Kambiri Victory High School does also believe Not All Students Are Born to be Doctors or Lawyers That is why we have and continue to promote a wide range of extracurricular activities that tap into every child talent and goals.

Academic

Not all Schools consider Academics as Extra-Curricular activities but at Victory We do and We train our students to understand and not merely pass the exams

Computer Studies

With Understanding of Importance of Having Computer Skills. At Victory High, We offer Computer Packages studies to All our staff and Students excelling in their Studies

Sports

While Our Program is Still Young. We are building a force to reckon with in Football(Soccer), Netball, Badminton, Rugby, Softball, Volleyball among others for both our boys and girls by tapping into their talents.

Chess

Chess is a strategy game for thinking people. Interested Student's at our school have the opportunity to learn the game.

Farming

In Partnership with Ushindi Children's Home who are striving for Self Sustainability. Top Performing students are offered the opportunity to learn successful farming techniques that include; proper planting, zero-grazing, poultry farming...

Music & Drama

As a center of cultural hub, Talented Students are offered the opportunity to showcase their talents through singing, reciting, acting, dancing among others
