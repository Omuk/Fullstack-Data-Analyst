Our Story

When a child’s parents die, he or she faces serious challenges which may include extremely limited to access of most basic needs like food, shelter, and clothing or being left with an abusive extended family, or a family that is too poor or one with other needs making taking the new child to be unwanted responsibility. In a lot of cases as in some testimonies from our children, the child may end up as a beggar, street child, prostitution, committing crimes like stealing to survive or performing child labor.

Such circumstance usually erodes any bright future the child might have and ensures continuity of the cycle of poverty.

Among the many visions for the local community, Bishop Jane Amunga and the Late Bishop Justus Amunga sought to end this vicious cycle by creating an environment where the children have a New Hope and access to A Shelter, Healthy Diet, Education and Spiritual wellbeing. That environment is the Ushindi Children’s Home Kenya.

At Our Home, the children are provided with a balanced and healthy diet of three meals a day. Every child has his or her own bed, beddings, and secured area to keep personal treasures. Each child has access to routine medical care as well as any care that is need.

The Children have access to quality education in institutions established by our founders and encouraged to participate in extracurricular activities that nurtures their talents provided in the schools. The children are raised in a safe, clean, loving environment where they can grow and flourish. As the Late Bishop Always believed, so do we:

The Future of Humanity is Dependent on How The Children Are Raised

Our Visions

We Exist to meet the needs of the orphans, Abandoned, Neglected or Abused children by providing education, food, shelter, clothing, health care, skills training and Christian discipleship

Our Mission
    To Win Orphans, Neglected, Abused or Abandoned Children to Christ by:
  1. Creating a Safe, nurturing, loving and Christian Home Environment for the children
  2. Meeting the needs of the Children by providing Education, Food, Shelter, Clothing, Health Care, Skills training and Christian discipleship
  3. Providing education to children that will help break the cycle of poverty in Kenya
  4. Providing Hope to Orphaned, Abandoned, Neglected or Abused Children by taking them off the streets
  5. Availing needed resources for the children to live a healthy and fulfilled life
