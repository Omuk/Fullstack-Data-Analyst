About Us

A Christian organization dedicated to building Self-Sustaining orphanages in Kenya to give new homes to children who have been orphaned - largely due to the HIV/AIDS pandemic or children who are Victims of Abuse, Neglect or Abandonment. In addition, we work to meet the needs of the poor and widowed

Our Vision

We Exist to meet the needs of the orphans, Abandoned, Neglected or Abused children by providing education, food, shelter, clothing, health care, skills training and Christian discipleship

Our Mission

We Exist to meet the needs of the orphans, Abandoned, Neglected or Abused children by providing education, food, shelter, clothing, health care, skills training and Christian discipleship and to Win their souls to Christ by:

  1. Creating a Safe, nurturing, loving and Christian Home Environment for the children
  2. Meeting the needs of the Children by providing Education, Food, Shelter, Clothing, Health Care, Skills training and Christian discipleship
  3. Providing education to children that will help break the cycle of poverty in Kenya
  4. Providing Hope to Orphaned, Abandoned, Neglected or Abused Children by taking them off the streets
  5. Availing needed resources for the children to live a healthy and fulfilled life

Why Join Us

Intermountain Christian School is The School For The Future. Our teaching methodology and culture is designed to not merely educate children but nurture them as well. In addition to the Curriculum required by the Education Department, we offer Extra-Curricular activities tailored towards providing success to our students through high school and Life.

Academic

Not all Schools consider Academics as Extra-Curricular activities but at Intermountain Christian School We do, and We train our students to understand and not merely pass the exams

Swimming Lessons

Students Attending Intermountain Christian School Are Offered an Opportunity in a safe environment to learn Swimming

Football

Also Known as Soccer, at our institution we are building a program that nurtures the children's talents in sports. Our Vision is to build both boys and girls Football Team that Conquerors all.

Volleyball

At our institution we are building a program that nurtures the children's talents in sports. Our Vision is to build both boys and girls Volleyball Team that Conquerors all.

Computer Studies

At Intermountain we offer our staff Computer Lessons to help ease the future transition of our students into computer studies. Our vision is to introduce Computer Programming as Part of the Program when God Provides Resources

Music & Drama

As a center of cultural hub, Talented Students at Intermountain Christian School are offered the opportunity to showcase their talents through singing, reciting, acting, dancing among others

Foreign Language

At Intermountain, we are trusting God in establishing a foreign Language Program for our student as the Lord Leads Us

Special Education

While not fully equipped to meet different special needs cases, We are trusting God for resources to be able to meet the need of caring for special needs children in our community

The Success of a Child is Highly dependent on the Academic and Talent Foundation. At Mountain We Strive to raise children with strong background to succeed in high school and later on in life.
