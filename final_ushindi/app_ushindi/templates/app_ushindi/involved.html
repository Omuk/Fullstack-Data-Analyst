GET INVOLVED

Ushindi Started as a Ministry preaching the gospel. It then expanded to catering for Orphans, Abandoned, Abused or Neglected Children. Shortly after, it expanded to other ministries that included provision of Quality education to communities. Throughout the Expansion, God has been faithful in providing Faithful Partners to his work. To get involved with supporting the Orphanage Please reach out to Our Partner AOO. For support of bright but poor students please reach out to VALOR. For any other inquiries please reach out to us @ email. If you need Prayers, the Bible says God Hears the Prayers of the Children and We do Have an Army of Prayer Warriors
