Get Involved

Ushindi Started as a Ministry preaching the gospel in Kambiri, Kenya. It then expanded to catering for Orphans, Abandoned, Abused or Neglected Children. Shortly after, it expanded to other ministries that included provision of Quality education to communities and ministering to widows and widowers. Throughout the Expansion, God has been faithful in providing Faithful Partners to His work. To get involved with supporting the Orphanage, please reach out to Our Partner African Orphan Outreach. For support of bright but poor students please reach out to Valor Global Foundation. For any other inquiries or if you need Prayers from Our Might Army of Prayer Warriors, please free to Reach Out To Us.

African Orphan Outreach

A Christian humanitarian response to the orphan crisis in Africa. AOO exists to connect people who want to share the love of Christ by providing a home and care and education for orphans amid the AIDS crisis in West Kenya.

Valor International Christian

A Christian Organization with a mission to break the chains of cyclical poverty through transformative education. It our hope to provide education for every student and equip them to put courageous faith into action.
