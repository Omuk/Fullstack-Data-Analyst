About Us

A religious non profit with a mission of spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ through preaching and acts of showing compassion to those in need.

We exists to meet the needs of the Orphans, Abandoned, Neglected, Abused or children, the poor and widowed. Goals are met through building Self-Sustaining orphanages, provision of nutritious food, clothing, shelter, skills training and christian discipleship

Our Story

In 1987, Our Founders Bishop Justus and Jane Amunga Got Married. After Receiving the Lord, Bishop Justus was so excited and sought to share the message in the community. Witnessing the struggles, the Orphans and Widowers undergo, they listened and began trusting God for His appointed time of Answering the Call.

In 2001 the Ushindi Children’s Home was Established with 81 kids. Ever since, the home has offered food, shelter, quality primary and secondary education, clothes, and healthcare to over 500 children. In 2012 and with only 10 students, the founders established Victory High Academy, a school offering affordable yet high quality education to students.

Intermountain Christian School (ICS) was established in 2015 to offer Affordable yet High Quality Education to children in the Community. Starting with only 2 students, the school has expanded to cater for over 500 students annually.

In 2020, Bishop Justus Amunga, The Ministries Founder, A man of Great Faith and a Faithfull Servant of God with a character of Christ of ministering to the needy, After he had served the Purpose of God in His generation fell asleep and was laid to rest in His Home.

Part of His Legacy is ministering to thousands through preaching the Gospel of Christ and through his charity work that has transformed communities. Over 500 to be hopeless children But Now Successful in Life Adults. In Addition, He authored Two Books which are available on demand on God’s Grace titled ‘The Power of God to Save’ & ‘Unfettered Access’.
