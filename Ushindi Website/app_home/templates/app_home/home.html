ABOUT US

A Christian organization dedicated to building Self-Sustaining orphanages in Kenya to give new homes to children who have been orphaned - largely due to the HIV/AIDS pandemic or children who are Victims of Abuse, Neglect or Abandonment. In addition, we work to meet the needs of the poor and widowed

Our Vision

We Exist to meet the needs of the orphans, Abandoned, Neglected or Abused children by providing education, food, shelter, clothing, health care, skills training and Christian discipleship

Our mission

    To Win Orphans, Neglected, Abused or Abandoned Children to Christ by:
  1. Creating a Safe, nurturing, loving and Christian Home Environment for the children
  2. Meeting the needs of the Children by providing Education, Food, Shelter, Clothing, Health Care, Skills training and Christian discipleship
  3. Providing education to children that will help break the cycle of poverty in Kenya
  4. Providing Hope to Orphaned, Abandoned, Neglected or Abused Children by taking them off the streets
  5. Availing needed resources for the children to live a healthy and fulfilled life
